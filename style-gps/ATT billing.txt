Ricky: To start this request, please click the View BAN Invoice Summary link on the left. Then click the Select link to select your billing account if it is not already selected. Then this will be your most important step. Next, please click the Billing Period drop down on the upper, right side to change your billing period to the one you need. Then, click the Download Account Bill Image (PDF) button. The “Account Billing Image with Usage Details” box will display as an option. Finally, complete the process by clicking the Download Bill Image PDF button.
